About Us

Poshan Care was founded in 2025 with a simple yet powerful mission: to fight childhood malnutrition and ensure every child has access to nutritious meals. Our journey began with a small team of passionate individuals who believed that no child should go hungry. Today, we work tirelessly to provide life-saving nutrition, promote healthy eating habits, and empower communities to break the cycle of malnutrition.

We are driven by the belief that every child deserves a chance to thrive. Through partnerships, donations, and community programs, we have impacted thousands of lives and continue to expand our reach.

Our Team

Our team is made up of dedicated professionals, volunteers, and advocates who are committed to making a difference. From nutritionists and healthcare workers to educators and community leaders, we work together to create sustainable solutions for childhood malnutrition.
